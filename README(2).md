## ⚡ What is Kaichi?

**Kaichi** is the innate ability to manipulate a single element from the
periodic table.

Each person can awaken only one elemental affinity, permanently forging
a bond that shapes their abilities, combat style, and identity.

The strength of a Kaichi user is determined not by their element, but by
their creativity, mastery, and willpower.

> *Your element does not determine your destiny---your imagination
> does.*
